Volleyball - Men

The Volleyball event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers an electrifying platform for players to showcase their skills, teamwork, and strategic play in this high-energy sport. Participants will have the opportunity to demonstrate their expertise in one of the most popular and dynamic team sports.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 6 players on the court, (6+6 substitutes)

  • Duration: Matches will follow a best-of-5 set format, with each set lasting approximately 10-15 minutes

  • Round Structure: Knockout format

  • Decision Process: Official refrees decision is final

  • No Violation : Protest at field will lead to disqualification

Notes and Requirements

  • Gameplay: Teams must demonstrate precise serving, passing, setting, attacking, blocking, and defense.

  • Strategy: A coordinated game plan with rotations and formations is essential for gaining an advantage.

  • Team Dynamics: Effective communication, smooth transitions, and substitution management drive team success.

  • Note1: Teams must bring their own gear, including jerseys and appropriate footwear

  • Note2: Certified referees will officiate, and their decisions are final.

  • Note3: Participants must follow the rules to ensure a fair and competitive environment.
