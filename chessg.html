Table Tennis

The Table Tennis event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites players to showcase their quick reflexes, precision, and strategy on the table. Players will engage in exciting matches, utilizing speed and control to outwit their opponents in this fast-paced sport.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 1 player per side, doubles is also allowed with 2 players per side

  • Duration: The match will be played in a best of 5 or 7 format, depending on the number of teams

  • Round Structure: Knockout or round-robin format depending on the number of registered teams

  • Decision Process: Time and Checkmate will be the decision point

  • No Violation : Protest at field will lead to disqualification

Notes and Requirements

  • Gameplay: Players must exhibit precision in strokes, effective ball placement, and consistency in rallies

  • Strategy: Tactical serves, returns, and game planning are essential for securing an advantage

  • Focus and Endurance: Maintaining concentration and physical stamina throughout the match will be critical

  • Note1: Participants must bring their own chessboard and clock if required; the organizers will provide these if unavailable

  • Note2: Matches will be officiated by experienced arbiters, and their decisions will be final

  • Note3: Players should be familiar with Blitz chess rules and standard chess regulations for fair play
