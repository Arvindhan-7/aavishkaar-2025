Channel Surfing

The Channel Surfing event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 challenges participants to perform a series of short, entertaining skits, each representing a different TV channel. Teams of four will showcase their creativity and humor through these skits, providing a fun and engaging experience for both participants and the audience. The event allows teams to explore different genres and themes of TV shows, requiring them to adapt quickly to different roles and situations.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 4 members per team

  • Time Limit: Each team will have 10 minutes for their performance

  • Format: Teams must perform a series of short skits, each representing a different TV channel

  • Theme: Open theme, but must be decent and respectful. Strictly no vulgar or inappropriate language or content

Notes and Requirements

  • Creativity: Teams should come up with original skits that represent various types of TV channels

  • Humor: Skits should be entertaining and humorous, engaging the audience effectively

  • Note1:One chance to perform for each team

  • Note2: Ensure that the skits are in line with the event's guidelines and appropriate for all audiences
