Solo Dance (FUSION)

The Solo Dance (Fusion) event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 challenges participants to showcase their dancing skills through a fusion of different dance forms. The event encourages creativity and innovation as participants blend various styles and genres to create a unique performance.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Time Limit: 2-4 minutes for the dance performance

  • Music: Participants must email their audio track to the AAVISHKAAR mail ID

  • Theme: Fusion (a blend of various dance styles and genres)

Requirements

  • Choreography: The dance routine should creatively combine different dance styles

  • Energy & Technique: The performance should demonstrate energy and precision

  • Expression: Participants should express emotions through movement


Note

  • One chance to perform for each participant

  • Participants must adhere to the theme of Fusion and be creative in blending various dance styles

Registration Fee: Rs.300 per Participant

Registration Deadline: March 1st, 2025
