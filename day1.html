"Advancements in sustainable smart engineering and technology (ASSET - 2025)"

The National Conference is an academic event where students present their research papers or technical ideas to a panel of judges, fostering knowledge-sharing and innovation. AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers a premier platform for presenting innovative research and ideas through the National Conference. Participants are invited to present their original research or technical concepts to an esteemed panel of judges.

  • Team Size: Maximum of 3 members per team
