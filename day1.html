National Conference 2025 - ASST

The National Conference is an academic event where students present their research papers or technical ideas to a panel of judges, fostering knowledge-sharing and innovation. AAVISHKAAR-2025 offers a premier platform for presenting innovative research and ideas through the National Conference. Participants are invited to present their original research or technical concepts to an esteemed panel of judges.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 members per team

  • Time Limit: Each team gets 8 minutes to present. Strict adherence to the time limit is required

  • Round Structure: Only one round with one presentation opportunity per team

  • Abstract Submission: All the Teams must submit their paper's abstract with this google form. Papers without abstracts will not be accepted

Notes and Requirements

  • A PowerPoint Presentation (PPT) is mandatory

  • The topic and PPT must be original. Plagiarism or AI-generated content is prohibited

  • The paper should follow the proper academic format for publication

  • Note1:One chance to present for each team

  • Note2:Ensure originality and academic rigor in your presentation
