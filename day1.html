Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: 3 members per team

  • Time Limit: Each team gets 8 minutes to present. Strict adherence to the time limit.

  • Round Structure: Only one round with one presentation per team.

  • Abstract Submission: All the Teams must submit their abstract through the Registration.

  • Abstract Template: For abstract template click here: TEMPLATE, Submission has to be in WORD Format.

Notes and Requirements

  • A PowerPoint Presentation (PPT) is mandatory

  • The topic and PPT must be original. Plagiarism or AI-generated content is prohibited

  • The paper should follow the proper academic format for publication.

  • Note 1: One chance to present for each team

  • Note 2: Ensure originality and academic rigor in your presentation
