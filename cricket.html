Cricket

The Cricket event at AAVISHKAAR-2025 provides a platform for cricket enthusiasts to showcase their skills in this exciting and action-packed format of the game. This event emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and individual brilliance, allowing participants to demonstrate their cricketing expertise in a competitive environment.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Each team can have a maximum of 11 players on the field and up to 4 substitutes (depending on the format)

  • Duration: Each match will consist of 5-6 overs per team, depending on the schedule and number of registered teams

  • Round Structure: Knockout or league format based on the number of registered teams

Requirements

  • Gameplay: Teams must exhibit batting, bowling, and fielding skills with precision and strategy

  • Strategy: Effective utilization of power plays, bowlers, and batting orders will be critical to success

  • Team Dynamics: Seamless collaboration among players, with efficient communication on and off the field, is vital


Note

  • Teams must bring their own cricketing equipment, including bats, protective gear, and team uniforms

  • Matches will be officiated by experienced umpires, and all decisions made during the match will be final

  • Ensure that all participants understand and comply with the rules and regulations of the game
