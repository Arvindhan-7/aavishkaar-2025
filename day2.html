Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Maximum of 3 members per team

  • Time Limit: Each team gets 10 minutes to present. Strict adherence to the time limit.

  • Round Structure: Only one round with one presentation per team.

  • Abstract Template: For abstract template click here: TEMPLATE, Submission has to be in WORD Format.

Notes and Requirements

  • Teams must display a standup model, working model, or product.

  • A PowerPoint Presentation (PPT) is optional.

  • Power supply and electricity will be provided on-site.

  • Teams must arrange the necessary components for their models or prototypes.

  • Note 1: One chance to present for each team

  • Note 2: Ensure the model is functional and displays technical process
