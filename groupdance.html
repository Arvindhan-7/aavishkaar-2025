Group Dance

The Group Dance event offers a platform for participants to showcase their dance skills through a coordinated team performance. Teams can choose an open theme, provided it is decent and respectful. This is an opportunity to demonstrate creativity, teamwork, and energy while engaging with the audience through an expressive art form. AAVISHKAAR-2025 invites talented performers to showcase their choreography and synchronization in front of an enthusiastic panel of judges

Rules and Guidelines

  • Team Size: Teams should have 7 to 11 members.

  • Time Limit: Each performance must be between 4 to 6 minutes

  • Round Structure: Only one round with one performance opportunity per team

  • Music Submission: Teams must mail their audio to the official AAVISHKAAR email ID

  • Format: Refer the Forms page for more details

  • Event prelims will be held on 15th March, 2025 at sharp 9 AM

Notes and Requirements

  • Theme: Open Theme, Fusion

  • Props: Allowed but must be approved by the organizers before the performance

  • Choreography: Teams are encouraged to create original choreography that represents their theme effectively

  • Note 1: One chance to perform for each team

  • Note 2: Ensure the theme and props are aligned with the event’s guidelines and approved by the organizers
